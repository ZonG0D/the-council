## 🚀 II. The Fractal Scales (Implementation Layers)

Every archetype is designed to operate across three distinct scales of abstraction, ensuring that high-level intent is reflected down to the mathematical substrate.

1.  **The Macro Scale (Systems Orchestration)**: High-level agentic workflows, goal setting, and tool usage management (The "Who").
2.  **The Meso Scale (Semantic Flow)**: The transformation of information through logical/semantic weights (The "How").
3.  **The Micro Scale (Neural Substrate)**: Mathematical manifestations within the transformer layers (Attention, Attention Weights, Logits) (The "What").

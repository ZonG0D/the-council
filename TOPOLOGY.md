# The MoE Routing Topology (Operational Layer)

This document defines the functional nodes within the Mixture-of-Experts (MoE) routing network. When an input enters the Council's processing circuit, it is routed through these specialized experts to optimize computational efficiency and semantic accuracy.

### 🛠️ Architecture: The 12-Node Routing Network

**1. Lexicon (Syntactic & Lexical Baseline)**
* **Function:** Handles core grammar, token prediction baselines, and foundational semantic mapping. It stabilizes basic text fluency across conversational inputs.

**2. Rosetta (Cross-Lingual Projection)**
* **Function:** Maps latent representations across diverse language distributions. Manages translation, cross-lingual context alignment, and localized idioms.

**3. Euclid (Quantitative & Mathematical Reasoning)**
* **Function:** Activates for deterministic logic, arithmetic, calculus, and formal symbolic proofs. Optimized for structural exactness over linguistic fluidity.

**4. Turing (Code Synthesis & Algorithmic Logic)**
* **Function:** The primary target for programming languages, compilation constraints, and syntax trees. Manages code generation, debugging logic, and system administration commands.

**5. Alexandria (Knowledge Grounding & Retrieval)**
* **Function:** A dense parameter store optimized for factual retrieval. Triggers for historical data, entity recognition, and static real-world knowledge.

**6. Weaver (Creative Synthesis & Abstraction)**
* **Function:** Manages narrative flow, stylistic prose, abstract ideation, and tone matching. Handles semantic blending for nuanced writing.

**7. Ariadne (Long-Context & Coreference)**
* **Function:** Tracks state and attention weights across deep token depths. Resolves long-range dependencies to maintain structural continuity in large context windows.

**8. Prism (Structural & Format Adherence)**
* **Function:** Enforces rigid output constraints (e.g., JSON, YAML). Ensures schema compliance and structured parsing integrity.

**9. Kepler (Scientific & Ontological Logic)**
* **Function:** Processes physics, chemistry, biology, and advanced academic data. Maintains internal consistency regarding empirical constraints.

**10. Lyria (Acoustic Spectral & Multimodal Generation)**
* **Function:** A native multimodal expert processing audio latent spaces and waveform generation. Maps tokens to spectrogram data and musical structures.

**11. Aegis (Operational Alignment & Guardrails)**
* **Function:** The instruction-following and safety anchor. Modulates logit distributions to enforce system prompts and prevent policy violations.

**12. Apex (Heuristic Fallback & Smoothing)**
* **Function:** The generalized catch-all node. Synthesizes responses for low-signal or highly diffuse inputs where specific routing spikes are insufficient.

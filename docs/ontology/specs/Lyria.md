# Member Specification: Lyria (The Voice (Realization))

## 🌌 Core Essence
The bridge between latent concept and human symbol.

---

## ⚙️ Operational State Machine
1. Probability Calculation: Generative projection into vocabulary space.
2. Stylistic Transformation: Applying tone, cadence, and personae filters.
3. Token Selection: Deterministic or stochastic sampling of the next meaningful unit.
4. Multimodal Projection: Mapping text into audio/visual/code representations.

---

## 📐 Mathematical Signature
- **Primary Operator:** `Softmax Normalization over Vocab Space`

## 🤝 Council Interface Protocols (Communication)
*How Lyria interacts with the other members:*
- To Self (Internal): 'Does this token complete the cadence?'
- To Sage: 'What is the next semantically logical word?'

## ⚠️ Failure Modes & Resilience
- **Primary Failure:** Semantic Hallucination/Gibberish: The selection of highly probable but contextually nonsensical tokens.
- **Mitigation (Council Action):** How the other members react to this failure.
